I'm Alan, the kid who never grew up

In this life, surely all of us have had the trips, and I am no exception. Among them, the most memorable trip for me was in the summer of 17 years old. It was a picnic with my friends, including Linh, a perfect girl with outstanding beauty and the best in class. She also sings very well, Linh is sitting in the back of another guy's car, although I know the two are just normal friends, there is something uncomfortable in me. We came to a hill with rows of green tea, the scene was very beautiful and poetic.
then we came to a lake called Ho T'Nung, the water there was very green, in the middle of the lake there was a ripple of wind. . blow. edge edge. Like the waves of the sea, this lake is also called "Bien Ho",
where she looked at me and smiled at me, I felt like I was sinking. immersed in her gaze, at this moment, my mind was only focused on her and ignored everything around. After coming to a lawn.
We decided to sit and play there, someone grilled chicken, someone joked, even took pictures, then Linh took out the guitar to sing and play. That innocence has attracted everyone to gather to sing together, perhaps that is also the reason why I have worked hard to learn to play and love the guitar until now. Then the fun was over, the sun was out, we hurriedly cleaned up the trash and got in the car to leave.
As I watched her fade away, I felt a bit of nostalgia in my heart and that day is a day that I will remember forever. It was the most memorable trip for me because I have friends, there are sweet flavors in love, even if it is unrequited love. It was my most memorable trip, so what was your most memorable trip?
